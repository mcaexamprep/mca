About MCA

About Master of Computer Application [MCA]:

  • The full form of MCA is Master of Computer Application, it is a Postgraduate Course in Computer Science and Application and the MCA course duration is for three years.

  • The programme is designed to provide students with a solid theoretical knowledge about Computer Application along with the practical application of the theoretical knowledge through MCA course subjects and syllabus.

  • The MCA course is inclined towards Application Development and thus has more emphasis on the latest programming language and tools to develop better and faster applications. 

  • The MCA course is intended to provide a modern, industry-oriented education in applied computer science. 

  • MCA course is a full-time degree course with a duration of three years (six semesters) covering various aspects of computational theory, programming, algorithm design and optimization, network and database management, mobile technologies, electronics, mathematics, probability, statistics, accounting, finance, etc.

  • As the IT and the software industry are dynamic and fast growing, all the MCA course programmes are designed keeping in view the requirements of the industry.

  • Students are trained in the fields of Systems Designing, Application Software Development, Enterprise Resource Planning, Computer Networks, System Administration, Web Designing and Development, Database Administration, Parallel and Vector Processing, Data Mining and Warehousing, etc for good MCA jobs & salary.

  • The MCA course prepares students to take up positions as System analysts, Systems designers, Programmers and Managers in any field related to information technology. The MCA course salary and job opportunities are high when compared with other fields of study.

  • The  MCA course, therefore, aims at imparting comprehensive knowledge with equal emphasis on theory and practice.

  • The students are encouraged to spend a full semester working in the industry in the institute giving them insight into the workings of the IT world as part of the MCA course programme.

  • MCA course is offered by Institutions all over the nation like Lovely Professional University, Jalandhar, Lal Bahadur Sastri Institute of Management, Delhi, Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Sciences, Mumbai etc and are among the top MCA colleges in India.
